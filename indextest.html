Bruno Faiotto

is a Brazilian graphic designer currently focused on the craft of 3D. He has a strong interest in creating contemplative eye-catching imagery, that takes the viewer to an almost surreal world but yet possible and tangible.


Graduated in graphic design at Universidade Estadual Paulista (UNESP) in 2019, he worked as a graphic designer at a studio in São Paulo for a year and then as full-time freelancer. After that, in 2022, he moved to London to join ManvsMachine.
