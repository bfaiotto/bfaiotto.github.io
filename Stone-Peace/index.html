Stone "Peace"


Stone it's a Brazilian company and one of the biggest credit card machine companies in Brazil and Latin America, with their machines present in a lot of shops.

To renew their imagery identity of products and have an image bank to use whenever they need it, they invited me to create some peaceful and elegant compositions with their products in similar ambients where they're used, like shop counters or bar tables.


Client: Stone
Year: 2021

Art Direction: Alexandre Rizzuti
3D & Art Direction: Bruno Faiotto
