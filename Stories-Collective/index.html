Stories Collective "Circular Innovation"


Stories Collective is a bilingual digital plataform of fashion content driven by topics that vary with each issue. Each publication gathers short stories, interviews, articles and fashion editorials.

In 2019 I was invited, along side Leonardo Bortolussi, to colaborate with the second printed edition, which theme was Imagining the Future, and illustrate the article "Circular Inovation in Textiles and Fashion".
