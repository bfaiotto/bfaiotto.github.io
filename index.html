Bruno Faiotto en / pt


is a Brazilian graphic designer currently focused on the craft of 3D. He has a strong interest in creating contemplative eye-catching imagery, that takes the viewer to an almost surreal world but yet possible and tangible.


After graduating in graphic design at Universidade Estadual Paulista (UNESP) in 2019, at the beginning of 2020, he returned to São Paulo and worked as a graphic designer at studio Rebu for a year and after that started his career as a full-time freelancer.
