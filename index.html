Bruno Faiotto is a brazillian designer focused in graphic design and 3D illustrations. With a strong interest in explore different paths and use 3D to create contemplative compositions that wouldn’t be possible to reach in real life, he seeks to craft eye-catching imagery that better fit each project.


After graduating in graphic design at Universidade Estadual Paulista in 2019, he moved back to his hometow and continues to freelance while work full time at REBU.
