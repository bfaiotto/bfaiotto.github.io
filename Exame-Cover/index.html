Exame
"O Futuro Chegou"


Exame it's a brazilian magazine focused in economy, tecnology, consumption, finance and others related topics. In 2021, I was invited to create an illustration for the cover and main article "O futuro chegou (The future arrived)", about bitcoin and the era of digital money.

The idea was to show the cryptocurrencys link with the digital world with coins rising from screens and pass the sensation of how volatile and no palpable the criptocurrency can be.
