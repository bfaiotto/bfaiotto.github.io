Emartes


The Escola Municipal de Artes (Municipal Art School) was concevied in 2019 from the Divisão de Ensino às Artes (Division of Arts Education), which operates inside the Secretaria de Cultura de Bauru (Bauru's Cultural Administration). It arose with the goal of expanding the free art lessons in the city through four main fields: Music, Visual Arts, Theater and Dance, respectively represented by the colors blue, green, yellow and red.

In 2018 we had the opportunity to build the school's identity from scratch, seeking to develop an identity that keep itself modern and up to date for a long time, but still invinting to all ages. Because of that we choose and sans serif font with rounded corners and we represented the four school fields through the colored "little balls" that unfolds into a grid system inside the identity.
